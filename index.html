How the @daily schedule works locally: Both airflow webserver --port 8080 and airflow scheduler must be running in separate terminals. The scheduler watches for the next 24-hour interval and fires the DAG automatically. The pipeline can also be triggered manually anytime via the ▶ button in the UI at localhost:8080.

Real anomalies on July 2: ethereum and solana both moved more than 5% vs. the previous stored values. The pipeline preserved both rows with is_anomaly=True rather than silently dropping them — anomalous data is flagged, not deleted, in financial systems.
Why FX rates look identical across some days: The ECB publishes exchange rates once daily at ~15:00 UTC. Runs before that time return the previous business day's rates. Crypto prices (bitcoin, ethereum, solana) update in real time via CoinGecko, so they show genuine movement every day.
